Trip Sage — Packing checklist (master reference)
=================================================

Carry-on
- [ ] Passport (valid 6+ months) + printed visa if required
- [ ] Travel insurance documents (refundable trip cover)
- [ ] Universal power adapter (Type A/B for Japan)
- [ ] Phone + chargers + portable battery (<= 100 Wh)
- [ ] Refillable water bottle
- [ ] Comfort items for long-haul (eye mask, earplugs, socks)

Clothing (3–10 day trips, layerable)
- [ ] Base layers x4
- [ ] Mid layer (fleece or light wool)
- [ ] Light waterproof shell
- [ ] Comfortable walking shoes (broken in)
- [ ] Smart-casual outfit x1

Partner-specific
- [ ] Confirm vegetarian options researched for each booked restaurant
- [ ] Snack pack for transit (vegetarian)

Booking checks
- [ ] All fares/stays flagged REFUNDABLE before payment
- [ ] Window seats selected for long-haul legs
